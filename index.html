About Kolhapuri Khajina

We are more than just a souvenir shop. We are storytellers and curators of culture. Our "Khajina" (Treasure) is a carefully handpicked collection of items that represent the heart and soul of Kolhapur.

From the iconic leather chappals to the intricate designs of the Kolhapuri Saaj, every item in our store has a story to tell. We started this shop to give visitors and locals alike a chance to find and take home a piece of our rich heritage. We believe in supporting local artisans and preserving the traditions that make Kolhapur so special.
